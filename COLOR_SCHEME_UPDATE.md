## Visual Hierarchy

The new color scheme maintains proper visual hierarchy:

1. **Background** (darkest/lightest) - Sets the stage
2. **Cards** (lighter in dark mode, white in light mode) - Content stands out
3. **Borders** (medium gray) - Subtle separation
4. **Accent** (yellow #FFB300) - Remains unchanged for consistency
